## Why I Built This

I built ShortLink AI to learn and demonstrate real-world backend development concepts including:

- Flask web development
- Database management with SQLite
- URL routing and redirects
- Dynamic web applications
- Frontend and backend integration

The project is being developed entirely on Android using Termux, showcasing a mobile-first development workflow.

## Problem Statement

Long URLs are difficult to share, remember, and track. They often look unprofessional in emails, social media posts, marketing campaigns, and business communications.

Many free URL shortening services provide limited customization, limited analytics, or require users to rely on third-party platforms.

ShortLink AI aims to solve this problem by providing a simple and modern platform where users can:

- Convert long URLs into short and shareable links
- Improve the appearance of links in messages and posts
- Track link performance and clicks (upcoming feature)
- Manage multiple shortened links from a single platform (upcoming feature)
- Generate QR codes for easier sharing (upcoming feature)

The goal is to build a lightweight, user-friendly URL management platform with analytics and productivity features.
